Subjects

Coding
Our first subject is coding.

Coding is one the essentials for robotics. It is taught by a higly experienced teacher. Every year a group of students taking the coding class are chosen to code the school robot for a competiton.




Animation
Our second subject is animation.

Animation is an enjoyable part of our program. We do stop motion as well as other types.





Robot Creation
Our third subject is Robot creation.

Robot creation is something that needs a lot problem solving. We want kids to build this ability.
